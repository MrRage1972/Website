The Founder's Story

Built by Someone Who Runs the Race

"I built MySecretary™ because I was tired of losing great ideas to the friction between thinking and executing. Executives shouldn't have to slow down to write emails — they should just talk."

I didn't build MySecretary™ from a quiet office or a startup incubator.

I built it while managing some of the busiest nightlife and restaurant environments in Los Angeles — working 10 to 12 hour shifts, moving nonstop, solving problems in real time, managing staff, handling guests, making connections, and constantly thinking three steps ahead.

Like most operators, I lived inside organized chaos.

And every day, ideas were getting lost.

Important contacts. Business opportunities. Follow-ups. Creative concepts. Tasks I needed to circle back to. I would either be too busy to write them down, or I'd throw them into a notes app that quickly became impossible to organize.

The problem wasn't a lack of effort.
The problem was friction.

Most productivity apps were built for people sitting at desks with time to slow down. But operators, founders, managers, creators, and entrepreneurs don't work that way. We move fast. We think fast. And when your workflow can't keep up with your mind, opportunities disappear.

After losing countless ideas and realizing no app truly solved the problem, I decided to build the system I wished existed.

That became MySecretary™.

A voice-first productivity platform designed for people in motion — built to turn thoughts into action instantly. From organizing notes automatically to generating summaries, outlines, to-do lists, calendar events, and even fully drafted emails, MySecretary™ was designed to remove friction from execution.

What started as a personal solution became something much bigger:
Executive software for faster execution.

Today, MySecretary™ is the foundation of the growing MySuite™ ecosystem under RNV-TECH, software built for founders, operators, creators, and high-performance individuals who refuse to let ideas get lost.
