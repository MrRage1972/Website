Privacy Policy

RNV-TECH LLC — MySecretary: Voice to action

1. Overview

MySecretary: Voice to action is a voice-first productivity app developed by RNV-TECH LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy explains what data we collect, where it lives, and how it is used. We built this app with a privacy-first approach — your notes, voice recordings, and photos never touch our servers.

2. Data We Collect

2a. Account Information (stored on our servers)

When you create an account, we collect and store:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (optional)
  • A hashed (encrypted) password — we never store your password in plain text
  • Authentication tokens used to keep you signed in

2b. Usage Logs (stored on our servers)

Each time you use an AI feature, we log:

  • Which AI mode was used (e.g., Summarize, Clean Up, To-Do)
  • The number of tokens processed (a measure of text length — not the text itself)
  • The AI model used and a timestamp

We do not log or store the content of your notes, voice recordings, or photos on our servers.

2c. Note Content, Voice Recordings & Photos (stored on your device and iCloud only)

All notes, audio recordings, and photos you create in the app are stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if you are signed into iCloud) — synced between your own devices only, not accessible to us. We cannot access your notes, recordings, or photos.

2d. App Preferences (stored on your device only)

Settings such as your chosen AI model, calendar provider, and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us.

3. Third-Party Services

3a. Anthropic (Claude AI)

When you use an AI feature (Summarize, Clean Up, To-Do, Email Draft, Outline, or image scanning), your note text and any attached photos are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. This is how the AI works. RNV-TECH does not store this content, but it is transmitted to and processed by Anthropic. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

3b. Supabase

We use Supabase to manage user accounts and authentication. Your email, hashed password, and session tokens are stored in Supabase's infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy at supabase.com/privacy.

3c. Google / Microsoft (Optional)

If you connect Google Calendar or Outlook Calendar, you will be asked to authorize access through OAuth. We store only your OAuth access token on your device (in the iOS Keychain) to perform calendar actions you request. We do not store your Google or Microsoft credentials.

4. How We Use Your Data

DataPurpose
Email & account infoAccount creation, login, password reset
Usage logsMonitoring API costs, detecting abuse, improving the service
Note content (in transit to Anthropic)Generating your requested AI output
OAuth tokensAdding events to your calendar on your request

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Usage logs are retained for up to 12 months
  • Notes, recordings, and photos are retained on your device and iCloud until you delete them — we have no copy

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You may request at any time:

  • Access to the data we hold about you
  • Correction of your account information
  • Deletion of your account and all associated data

To delete your account: Open MySecretary: Voice to action → Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, email, and usage logs from our servers. Notes on your device and iCloud are not affected and must be deleted manually.

You may also contact us at support@rnv-tech.com for any data requests.

7. Children's Privacy

MySecretary: Voice to action is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us at support@rnv-tech.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain (encrypted, device-only)
  • All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS
  • Your note content is processed in transit and not written to any database on our infrastructure

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will notify users of material changes via the app or email. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
