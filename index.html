The Founder's Story

Built by Someone Who Runs the Race

"I built MySecretary™ because I was tired of losing great ideas to the friction between thinking and executing. Executives shouldn't have to slow down to write emails — they should just talk."

Michael Bettis is the founder and CEO of RNV-TECH LLC. He started the company with a single belief: the best software is invisible — it just gets out of your way and lets you execute.

With a background in operations and digital product development, Michael built MySecretary™ for the version of himself that had too many ideas and not enough time to act on them all. The result is a product that thinks like an executive and moves like a machine.
