The Founder's Story

Built by Someone Who Runs the Race

"I built MySecretary: Voice to action because I was tired of losing great ideas to the friction between thinking and executing. Executives shouldn't have to slow down to write emails — they should just talk."

Michael Bettis is the founder and CEO of RNV-TECH LLC. He started the company with a single belief: the best software is invisible — it just gets out of your way and lets you execute.

He built MySecretary: Voice to action while managing some of the busiest nightlife and restaurant environments in Los Angeles — living inside organized chaos, watching great ideas get lost to friction every single day. So he built the system he wished existed.
